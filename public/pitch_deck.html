⚠️ The Retail Investor's Barrier

Analysis Paralysis & Intimidation

  • Complexity Overload: Sifting through hundreds of raw stock sheets is tedious.
  • Cognitive Strain: Ratios like P/E, EPS, ROE, and Debt are confusing for laymen.
  • Execution friction: Deploying portfolios requires manual fractional calculations.
📉

💡 Enter StockMatch

A Tactile, Gamified Matching Environment

  • Swipe Loop: Swiping deck with Outfit font layouts and interactive month selectors.
  • English Translations: Ratios on card backs are auto-checked for risk (Red) vs. safety (Green) thresholds with layman tips.
  • Fractional-Free Sandbox: Integrates whole shares calculations and refunds remaining budget cents.
  • Zero Management Fees: Empowering users to build custom, direct stock portfolios and completely bypass expensive fund management fees.
🔥

⚙️ Award-Winning Features

📊
Multi-Select Compare
Select multiple stocks to analyze in a side-by-side matrix showing 10 variables, including custom risk metrics.
🔄
Live IBKR Ingestion
Connects to a local IBKR Client Portal gateway and polls real-time prices & spreads with live terminal pipelines.
🎟️
Integer Buy Orders
A simulated order ticket screen showing quantity buy logs, actual capital spend, and refund cash remainder.

  • Frontend Core: React 18, Vite build system, Framer Motion for swiping physics.
  • Design System: Stitch Premium Glassmorphism theme (frosted glass layers, active outlines).
